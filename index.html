My Maker Coin

I modeled my own maker coin in Autodesk Fusion and animated an orbiting camera for presentation. The CAD file is both machinable and FDM 3D printable. I composed, produced, and mastered the music in Ableton Live and edited the video using CapCut.

Currently, I'm developing my own linear robotic actuator and performing a spatial kinematics study on one of ABB's cobots, the CRB 1100. My plan is to use these analyses for a homebrewed FDM printed cobot, and to optimize other machines for precision in manufacturing.
